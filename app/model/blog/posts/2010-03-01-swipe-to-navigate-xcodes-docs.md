title: "Swipe to navigate Xcode's docs"
---

As of recently, Xcode's built-in documentation browser lets you swipe left or right with 3 fingers to navigate it, just like Safari does. Let's hope my acquaintance Alex implements this same feature into [his app Ingredients](http://github.com/fileability/Ingredients), which looks like a really cool alternative to Xcode's doc browser.
