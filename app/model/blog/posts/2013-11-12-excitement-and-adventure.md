---
title: "Excitement and Adventure"
---

I love programming. I love solving really interesting and fun problems. I love coming up with new and creative solutions for them! I love seeing the pieces just come together exactly as planned, and seeing my software just work!

It's like an adventure. It's like discovering a new island and exploring it and building a new city on it! You get to use all the parts of your brain at once. You're constantly learning, constantly creating and discovering.

But as it turns out, it's rare to find people in real life that feel the same way about the same things.

Even when I worked with a few dozen really passionate programmers, there usually was no overlap in our interests. But it makes sense. Programming is a really huge field with many different sub-fields, and they can be really far removed from each other.

So instead, I just write about these adventures here, on my blog. I'm writing it to you. Maybe you find these adventures exciting too.
