Further thoughts on GUIs I'm not sold on the whole Reactive Functional Programming model yet, whether in web (via React or whatever) or on the desktop. People still seem to be trying to figure out the best way to make that model work, since, even though it makes some things easier, it makes other things harder. For now, I prefer to always use the Path Of Least Resistence ™ on the given platform. In web, that means mostly just ordinary HTTP requests with a little bit of jQuery stuff as-needed. On desktop, that means just using whatever that platform makes easiest, in Apple's case Xcode & Swift.
