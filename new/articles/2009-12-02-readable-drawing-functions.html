Readable drawing functions If you do any drawing with Cocoa, and don't already know about the [Foundation Functions Reference](http://developer.apple.com/mac/library/documentation/Cocoa/Reference/Foundation/Miscellaneous/Foundation%5FFunctions/Foundation%5FFunctions.pdf), you're going to want to check it out. Many handy functions are in there that will greatly enhance the readability of your code, such as NSDivideRect, NSMinX, NSHeight, NSOffsetRect, NSInsetRect, etc. Check out that whole section with those functions in it for more incredibly useful functions dealing with NSRects, NSPoints, NSSizes, and NSRanges.
