Strange alternative to Express.js The route `/blog/:slug` is stupid. All blog posts are known ahead of time. There's no need to check the DB for slugs like `obviously-not-a-real-slug` or `qasfdhas;ldfasl;` or `1111111111` at all. Absolutely no need. So I made a new alternative to Express.js called [Novo Cantico](https://novocantico.org/) that creates all routes and just adds them to a map. It's *way* cool. Please sponsor it on GitHub.
