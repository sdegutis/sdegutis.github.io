Respect Let's **be respectful** to one another, always treating each other with kindness. When we get a nasty email, let's give that person the benefit of the doubt and assume they're just having an off day. Let's keep politics and religion out of the picture. We probably won't agree on a given topic, and they're emotionally charged topics, but **we still have to work together**. Bringing these things up is more likely to hurt relationships than to do any good. Let's be respectful to **everyone**, regardless of their religion, political views, stance on LGBT issues, ethnicity, sex, hair color, favorite programming language, whatever. Let's respect each other's **boundaries**, both physical and otherwise. It's nice to have your own little bubble and rest assured it won't be invaded. So let's do our part to make sure everyone can enjoy that! Let's not make jokes at work that we wouldn't make around other people's children. We don't need to use innuendo in our humor, and we don't need to put other people down to get a laugh. There are plenty of **punny** jokes that are respectful and considerate. We can do this! It's not that hard. And it will make the world a **better place**.
