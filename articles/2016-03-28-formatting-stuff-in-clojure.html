Formatting stuff in Clojure, with no external dependencies ```clojure ;; Commas (format "%,d" 10000000000) ;; "10,000,000,000" ;; Rounding to two decimal points (format "%.2f" 1234.5678) ;; "1234.57" ;; Rounding to whole numbers (format "%,.0f" 1234.56) ;; "1,235" ;; Referring to parameters (format "%1$s %2$s %1$s" "run" "spot") ;; "run spot run" ;; Short dates (format "%1$tb %1$te, %1$tY" (java.util.Date.)) ;; "Mar 28, 2016" ``` For more fun with `clojure.core/format`, look at the top of the Javadoc page for `java.util.Formatter`.
