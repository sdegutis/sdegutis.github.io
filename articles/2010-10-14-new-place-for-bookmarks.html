New Place for Bookmarks My Bookmarks bar in Chrome (and previously Safari) got way out of hand. There were recursive folders and hundreds of link, which I never read. Recently I accidentally deleted all of them, too, so that kind of sucked. But a few days before I did that, I had an idea: instead of bookmarking things in the app, I would simply drag links to my Desktop! So far I have [18 bookmarks on my Desktop](http://cl.ly/04c0ac25407169fd778d) (with the help of my app DeskLabels to label groups of related bookmarks). The surprising thing is that this number is steadily staying the same! This is because, since the bookmarks are in plain view, I'm reminded of them a lot more frequently. So instead of browsing for a random article on Wikipedia, I'm a lot more likely to click one of these links. Then, when I've read it, I just delete the link, freeing up space. It's such a sweet system. I read a lot more often and have a lot less bookmarks to "get to, eventually."
