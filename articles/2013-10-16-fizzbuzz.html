FizzBuzz Found this puzzle recently. The idea is to take the numbers 1 to 100 inclusive, and for each number, print: * "fizz" if it's divisible by 3 * "buzz" if it's divisible by 5 * "fizzbuzz" if it's divisible by 15 * Otherwise, the number itself. Here'e my solution: ```clojure (defn fizzbuzz [n] (cond (zero? (mod n 15)) :fizzbuzz (zero? (mod n 3)) :fizz (zero? (mod n 5)) :buzz :else n)) (->> (range 1 101) (map fizzbuzz) (map println) (doall)) ``` It meets these requirements, although it also prints some extra junk at the end. Granted, the requirements didn't say not to! I feel kind of like I cheated with the idea of using `cond` though, since I saw it in an existing solution. But the rest of it is mine :)
